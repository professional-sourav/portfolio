About Me

I am Sourav Chakraborty, a Full-Stack Developer from India with over 8 years of experience in software and web development. My core strengths lie in Laravel, WordPress, and API-based architectures. I’ve contributed to backend-heavy teams, developing high-traffic APIs and integrating with third-party services like Twilio, MailGun, SendGrid, Shopify, and Zoho.

I have deep experience building custom WordPress plugins and themes using Elementor, Divi, Thrive, and custom frameworks. I’ve extended functionality in major plugins like Yoast and developed complete solutions for WooCommerce-based e-commerce sites.

In Laravel, I’ve handled job queues, real-time broadcasting with Pusher, Redis caching, and robust service-based application design. I follow the Service pattern regularly and am also comfortable with Repository structures. Advanced features like Laravel Horizon, Events, Observers, Morphing, and Batch processing are part of my everyday toolkit.

I’ve solved challenges like real-time iframe-based content loading, scraping with fallback services like ScrapeBee, and X-Frame security issues. I also work with Python and Node.js for data handling and scripting, and prefer Tailwind CSS for clean frontend design.
